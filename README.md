## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a chapter from the perspective of the shipping agent with the waxed mustache, detailing the strange cargo he's seen cross the country.
- Compose the field notes of Professor Thorne as he dissects the passenger pigeon, his scientific observations mingling with a growing dread.
- Write a monologue by Cornelius Lattimore addressed to the portrait of a business rival, explaining why his new palace needs a gallery of extinct birds.
- Create the oral story Mato's father tells upon returning from the hunt, describing the 'confused, diminished streams' of the buffalo.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map tracing the routes of the market hunters' telegraph messages and the railroad lines that shipped the barrels of pigeons.
- Create a data visualization of the logarithmic decline of the passenger pigeon population against the linear growth of railroad track mileage, 1850-1880.
